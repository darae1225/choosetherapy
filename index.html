Jerome Choo

Jerome graduated from Curtin University with a Bachelor of Occupational Therapy with honors. He has extensive experience working as an Occupational Therapist in the NDIS services and provided community rehabilitation to people in their homes.

Jerome enjoys the therapeutic process of getting to know his clients, discovering their goals and drawing upon her knowledge, creativity and determination to help his clients achieve them. Jerome is passionate about social participation and equality and will advocate for his clients at every opportunity.
